House Rules
Sports X Arena · Member Agreement
⏱ Booking Times
  • Start and finish within your booked slot.
  • Extra time only allowed if the court is free.
  • Late arrivals do not extend booking time.
  • No barefoot play or unsafe footwear permitted.
🤝 Player Conduct
  • Respect staff, coaches, and fellow players.
  • No abusive language, fighting, or dangerous behaviour.
  • Unsportsmanlike conduct may lead to suspension.
🏟 Court Care
  • Do not damage glass walls, nets, or fencing.
  • No hanging on nets or hitting walls intentionally.
  • Members are liable for damages caused intentionally.
⚠️ Cancellation Policy
You have 1 hour after booking to cancel free of charge. After that, your payment is non-refundable.
